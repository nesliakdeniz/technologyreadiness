Ag Technology: Are You Ready?

A worksheet to assess your readiness to adopt new technology

Adopting new technology can be a big investment. When done well, it can improve efficiency and profits. When done poorly, it can be costly. Rate each statement below. Your category averages plot on the Technology Readiness Wheel as you go, and any category that averages 3 or lower is flagged as an area that may benefit from further learning, planning, or support.

Rate each statement: 1 Not at all 2 Minimally 3 Moderately 4 Sufficiently 5 Completely
