This application was developed with the assistance of an AI tool, which was used to write the underlying code (HTML, CSS, and JavaScript). The content is not AI-generated: the nine readiness categories, the 27 statements, the 1-to-5 rating scale, the threshold of 3, and the guiding questions all come from the Ag Technology Readiness worksheet, and the linked resources are published Extension materials.
